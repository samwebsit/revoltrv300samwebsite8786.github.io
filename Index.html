  • About Revolt RV300 Bike

Revolt RV 300 is an electric street bike available at a starting price of Rs. 1,08,648 in India. It is available in only 1 variant and 2 colours. Revolt RV 300 generates 1500 W power from its motor. With both front and rear disc brakes, Revolt RV 300 comes up with combined braking system of both wheels. Based on the RV 400, Revolt Motors has also introduced its scaled-down version in the form of the RV 300. Available in only one variant, the RV 300 follows a similar design language like its elder sibling following the same street bike stance.

The RV 300 is available with the choice of two colour shades, Smokey Grey and Neon Black. The RV 300 is powered by a 1.5KW hub-mounted motor that draws charge from 60V/2.7 kWh lithium-ion battery. The battery takes 3 hours for a 75 per cent charge and 4.2 hours for a complete charge. Revolt has equipped the RV 300 with three riding modes- Eco, Normal and Sport. The top speed and range vary depending on the mode the motorcycle is ridden. The RV300 is equipped with a digital instrument cluster and full-LED lighting. It also gets an app which can be used with the motorcycle for features like geofencing, notification when the battery is low besides several other features.

For cycle parts, the RV 300 features inverted forks up front and an adjustable monoshock for the rear while braking is done by a disc-drum arrangement which is equipped with CBS. The motorcycle rides on 17-inch alloy wheels shod with 90/80 (front) and 110/80 (rear) section tyres.
